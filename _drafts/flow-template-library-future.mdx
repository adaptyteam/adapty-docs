## Generate a flow with AI

To draft a flow from a natural-language description, use the **Generate paywall using AI** field at the top of the Template library. Type a short description of the flow you want, then click **Generate**.

Adapty drafts an editable flow that matches your description. You can refine it in the builder like any other flow.

## Create a flow from a screenshot

To recreate a design as a flow, click **Create from Screenshot**. Upload a screenshot of the design you want to reproduce.

Adapty analyzes the screenshot and builds an editable flow that matches the layout. Refine it in the builder.

## Copy a design from one of your apps

If you have multiple apps in your account, you can reuse a design from one app in another. Click **Copy a design from your app** in the Template library, then pick the source app and flow. Adapty loads the flow into your current app as a new draft.

The original flow in the source app is unaffected.

## Browse individual screens

To pick screens instead of whole flows, switch to the **Screens** tab inside the Template library. Screens appear in a 4-column grid.

Hover a screen and click its checkbox to select it. You can select multiple screens at once. The top bar shows the running count and an **Open screens in builder →** button. Click it to add the selected screens to your current flow.

Selections carry over between the Flows tab and Screens tab in the same section. Switching between Library, Saved, and My templates resets the selection.

### Cross-tab screen selection

Screens picked from a flow row and screens picked from the Screens tab share the same selection pool. The top-bar counter always shows the total number of unique screens.

To pick a single screen from a flow, hover the screen card inside the flow row and click its checkbox. To pick the whole flow at once, hover the flow row header and click **Use as template**.

If you have only some screens of a flow selected, the flow row header shows a partial-selection indicator — for example, "9 / 12 selected".

A screen that is already in the selection appears pre-checked when you see it on the Screens tab. Clicking it removes it from the pool.

## Filter templates

The Template library offers filters in the left sidebar. The available filters depend on the active tab.

**Flows tab:**

- **Industry** — multi-select pills: Health & Fitness, Meditation & Mindfulness, Productivity, Photo & Video Utilities, Entertainment, Education, Travel, AI. Click **Show more** to reveal industries beyond the default visible count.
- **Flow length** — Quick (1–3 screens), Standard (4–7), Extended (8–15), Full funnel (16+).
- **Paywall** — toggle to show only flows that end with a paywall screen.
- **Theme** — All, Light, Dark.

**Screens tab:**

- **Industry** — same options as the Flows tab.
- **Products** — No products, 1 product, 1+ products.
- **Media** — All, Image, Video, None.
- **Theme** — All, Light, Dark.

Each filter group has a **Clear** link that resets only that group. The link appears only when at least one option in the group is active.

If your filters return no templates, the library shows: *No templates match your filters — Try adjusting or clearing your filters.* The sidebar stays visible so you can adjust without closing the modal. Click **× Clear filters** to reset every filter at once.

## Save templates for later

To bookmark a template, click the bookmark icon below the card. Bookmarked templates appear in the **Saved** section in the Template library's left sidebar.

A bookmark icon stays visible on a saved card at all times. On an unsaved card, the icon appears only on hover.

To save a whole flow at once, click the bookmark icon on the flow row header. Adapty saves every screen in that flow to the Saved section.

The Saved section has its own filters:

- **Type** — multi-select pills. Adapty shows only the types you have saved. If you have not saved any Loader screens, the Loader pill does not appear.
- **Sort by** — toggle between **Newest saved ↓** and **Oldest saved ↑**. The default is newest first.

If you have nothing saved yet, the section shows: *Nothing saved yet — Bookmark templates from the library to find them here faster.*

## Reuse templates from your own apps

If you manage multiple apps in your account, you can save flows or screens from one app and reuse them in another. Saved items appear in the **My templates** section in the Template library's left sidebar.

Each entry shows the source app's icon alongside the flow name. The **Apps** dropdown filters entries by source app and supports search for accounts with many apps.

You cannot open a template from My templates in its original builder — the original flow may be live in production or part of an A/B test. The only action is **Use as template**, which loads the design into your current app as a new draft.

The Sort by toggle works the same as in the Saved section: **Newest saved ↓** or **Oldest saved ↑**.

If you have not saved anything yet, the section shows: *No templates yet — Screens and flows you save from your apps will appear here.*

## Change a template in an existing flow

To swap the design of a screen in a flow you are already editing, click **Change template** on the active screen. The Template library opens with the **Screens** tab selected by default, since you are replacing a single screen rather than picking a new flow.

The **Start from Scratch** entry is renamed to **Add blank screen** in this context. Adding a blank screen inserts an empty screen at the current position instead of starting a new flow.
