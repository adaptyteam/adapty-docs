In this guide, you will set up Adapty Mail and launch your first email campaign. The setup has three parts: passing user emails to Adapty via SDK, verifying your sending domain, and configuring your checkout funnel. Once those are done, you can generate and launch a campaign. Plan for about 1 hour total — domain verification takes 10–30 minutes once DNS records are added; checkout configuration time varies.

## Before you start

- Your app already sends revenue events to Adapty (Adapty SDK installed). If not, complete the [quickstart guide](quickstart) or set up [observer mode](implement-observer-mode) first.
- Your app collects user emails and passes them to Adapty. If not, see [Collect user emails](mail-collect-emails) for strategies.
- You have access to your domain's DNS settings.
- You have a Stripe account with your subscription products configured (used inside the checkout funnel).

## 1. Pass user emails to Adapty

Adapty Mail needs two pieces of data per user: a stable identifier and their email address. If you already call `Adapty.identify()` in your app code, you only need to add the email step. Otherwise, complete both steps below.

### Identify your users

To identify users, call `Adapty.identify()` with a stable user ID — your backend user ID, Firebase UID, or similar. This ID links email clicks and purchases back to the correct Adapty profile.

- [React Native](react-native-identifying-users)

## 2. Set up your sending domain

Adapty Mail sends from your own domain. You add DNS records once — all campaigns use the same verified domain.

1. In Adapty Mail, go to **Settings → Domain**.
2. Enter your root domain (for example, `yourapp.com` — not a subdomain) and click **Continue**.
3. Adapty generates two sending subdomains and shows the required DNS records.
4. In your domain registrar, add the 10 DNS records shown (5 per subdomain):
   - 3 CNAME records (DKIM) per subdomain
   - 1 MX record (Mail-From) per subdomain
   - 1 TXT record (SPF) per subdomain
5. Optionally, add a DMARC TXT record on your root domain (recommended).
6. Return to **Settings → Domain** and click **Verify**.

Verification typically completes within **10–30 minutes**.

:::important
The verification window expires after **7 days**. If it lapses, your DNS records remain in place — return to **Settings → Domain** and re-enter your domain to start a new verification window.
:::

:::note
DNS changes can take up to 48 hours to propagate, though most registrars update within minutes.
:::

The DNS records table in Adapty Mail shows the exact values to copy into your registrar.

## 3. Set up checkout

Each email contains a unique checkout link per recipient. The funnel is built in **Web Paywalls** using FunnelFox, which is automatically provisioned when you log into Adapty Mail.

1. Go to **Web Paywalls → Create**.
2. Choose **Generate with AI** or enter an existing funnel URL.
3. Click **Open paywall editor** to open FunnelFox automatically.
4. In FunnelFox, connect your Stripe account and configure payment settings.
5. Click **Publish** in the FunnelFox editor to make the funnel live.

For details on the checkout funnel anatomy and how personalization works, see [Set up checkout](mail-checkout).

## 4. Create and launch your first campaign

1. In Adapty Mail, go to **Campaigns → Create**.
2. Paste your App Store URL (for example, `https://apps.apple.com/app/id...`).
3. Select a [tone preset](mail-tone-presets) and optionally set an email count (3–15).
4. Click **Generate**. Generation typically completes in under 2 minutes. If it fails or times out, check your internet connection and try again.
5. Preview each email; regenerate any that don't fit.
6. Click **Launch**.

See [Create a campaign](mail-create-campaign) and [Launch a campaign](mail-launch-campaign) for full details.

## Troubleshooting

| Issue | Solution |
|---|---|
| DNS verification stuck | Check that records match exactly — no trailing dots, correct CNAME targets. Wait 10 minutes, then click **Verify** again |
| Verification window expired | Your records remain in place. Re-enter your domain in **Settings → Domain** to start a new window |
| Generation failed or timed out | Check your internet connection and try again. If the issue persists, contact Adapty support |
