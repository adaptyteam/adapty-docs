The **[Charts](https://app.adapty.io/analytics/charts/)** tab shows your app's revenue, subscriptions, trials, and billing issues over time.

For descriptions and side-by-side comparisons of every chart, see the [Metric comparison table](metric-comparison-table). For filtering, grouping, and time-range controls, see [Analytics controls](controls-filters-grouping-compare-proceeds).

Paywalls, placements, and onboardings have their own metrics pages: [Paywall metrics](paywall-metrics), [Placement metrics](placement-metrics), and [Onboarding metrics](onboarding-metrics).

### Subscription and in-app charts

- [Revenue](revenue): Total revenue from subscriptions and one-time purchases, minus refunds.
- [MRR](mrr): Monthly recurring revenue from active subscriptions.
- [ARR](arr): Annual recurring revenue from active subscriptions.
- [ARPU](arpu): Average revenue per user.
- [ARPPU](arppu): Average revenue per paying user.
- [Installs](installs): Number of app installs.
- [Active subscriptions](active-subscriptions): Paid subscriptions that haven't expired.
- [New subscriptions](reactivated-subscriptions): Subscriptions activated for the first time.
- [Subscription renewal cancelled](cancelled-subscriptions): Subscriptions where auto-renewal was turned off.
- [Expired (churned) subscriptions](churned-expired-subscriptions): Subscriptions that ended.
- [Non-subscriptions](non-subscriptions): Count of consumables, non-consumables, and non-renewing subscriptions.

### Trial charts

- [Active trials](active-trials): Trials that haven't expired.
- [New trials](new-trials): Trials activated.
- [Trial renewal cancelled](trials-renewal-cancelled): Trials where auto-renewal was turned off.
- [Expired (churned) trials](expired-churned-trials): Trials that ended without converting.

### Billing and refund charts

- [Grace period](grace-period): Subscriptions that entered the grace period after a failed payment.
- [Grace period converted](grace-period-converted): Grace period subscriptions that successfully renewed.
- [Grace period converted revenue](grace-period-converted-revenue): Revenue from grace period recoveries.
- [Billing issue](billing-issue): Subscriptions that entered the billing issue state.
- [Billing issue converted](billing-issue-converted): Billing issue subscriptions that successfully renewed.
- [Billing issue converted revenue](billing-issue-converted-revenue): Revenue from billing issue recoveries.
- [Refund events](refund-events): Number of refunded purchases or subscriptions.
- [Refund money](refund-money): Total amount refunded.
