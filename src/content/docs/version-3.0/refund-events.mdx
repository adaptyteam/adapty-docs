The Refund events chart shows how many purchases and subscriptions were refunded. Adapty ties each refund event to the date the refund was issued, not to the subscription start date.

<ZoomImage id="a3e1945-small-CleanShot_2023-05-11_at_18.36.262x.webp" width="700px" alt="Refund events chart" />

## Calculation

Adapty counts every purchase or subscription refunded within the selected period. Each refund is attributed to the date it happened, not to when the subscription began. Refunds for trials are excluded because trials generate no revenue.

## Available filters and grouping

:::link

- ✅ Filter by: Attribution, Audience, Refund Reason, Country, Offer Type, Offer ID, Offer Discount Type, Paywall, A/B tests, Placement, Period, Segment, Store, Product, and Duration.
- ✅ Group by: Refund Reason, Product, Country, Store, Paywall, Audience, Placement, Duration, Offer Type, Offer Discount Type, Offer ID, Segment, and Attribution.
