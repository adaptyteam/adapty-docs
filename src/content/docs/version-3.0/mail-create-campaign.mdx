Adapty Mail generates an email sequence from your App Store listing — no copywriting or design work required. Provide an App Store link, choose a tone, and the AI produces a ready-to-send campaign in under 2 minutes.

## Generate a campaign

1. In Adapty Mail, go to **Campaigns → Create**.
2. Paste your App Store URL (for example, `https://apps.apple.com/app/id...`).
3. Select a [tone preset](mail-tone-presets) — options are generated specifically for your app's category and brand voice.
4. Optionally, set the number of emails (3–15; the AI defaults to approximately 7 based on your app category).
5. Click **Generate**.

While generating, the AI runs six steps automatically:

1. Analyzes your app's name, description, category, and screenshots from the App Store.
2. Extracts brand colors, logo, and typography from your Adapty project metadata.
3. Builds a color palette, dark mode version, and reusable HTML components.
4. Determines email count, purposes, timing, and layout for the sequence.
5. Generates copy for each email: 3 subject line variants, headline, body, and CTA.
6. Creates a hero image for each email and formats everything for major email clients.

The full process completes in under 2 minutes.

## Review your emails

After generation, preview each email in the sequence. You cannot edit email copy directly — to change an email, use **Regenerate**. For each email you can see:

- Subject line options (3 variants — all are tested automatically when you launch; see [A/B testing](mail-ab-testing))
- Headline and body copy
- Hero image
- CTA text
- Layout and purpose

## Regenerate an email

If an individual email doesn't fit — mismatched image, off-tone copy, or weak subject lines — you can regenerate it without affecting the rest of the sequence.

To regenerate an email:
1. In the campaign preview, open the email you want to change.
2. Click **Regenerate**.
3. The AI generates new copy and a new hero image for that email.

**What stays the same:**
- The email's position in the sequence
- The delay timing before it sends
- The design system (color palette, typography, dark mode)

**What changes:**
- All copy (subjects, headline, body, CTA)
- The hero image
- Possibly the email purpose and layout, based on the new content

:::note
The AI takes the surrounding sequence into account when regenerating. The new email is written to fit with the emails before and after it.
:::
