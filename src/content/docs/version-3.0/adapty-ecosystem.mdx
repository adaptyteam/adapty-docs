Adapty is an in-app purchase platform for mobile apps, built around one mission: make apps rich. It gives you everything to grow revenue: acquire users, convert them, keep them subscribed, and win back the ones who leave.

One signup gives you access to the entire Adapty ecosystem from day one. Just click the Adapty logo to switch between the products:

- **Core** — process purchases without touching StoreKit or Google Play Billing, design no-code paywalls, and track revenue in real time. The other products build upon this foundation.
- **Adapty Ads Manager** — run and optimize Apple Ads, measured against real subscription revenue.
- **Adapty Attribution** — see which ad channels actually drive revenue, no MMP required.
- **Adapty Mail** — convert trials and win back churned users with automated email.

Two more products sit alongside the core four: **FunnelFox** (web-to-app funnels and hosted checkout) and **Adapty Finance** (advances against future subscription revenue).

## Core

Core is the base Adapty platform. It presents your paywalls, oversees purchases, and measures what happens next. 

### SDKs and stores

Skip the billing plumbing. Adapty handles purchases, receipt validation, and renewals for you, and keeps every subscriber's status accurate in real time — so you always know who has access and why.

Inside your app, the [Mobile SDKs](installation-of-adapty-sdks) run the purchase flow end-to-end, or [observe your existing billing](observer-vs-full-mode) if you already have one. They cover 7 platforms: [iOS](ios-sdk-overview), [Android](android-sdk-overview), [React Native](react-native-sdk-overview), [Flutter](flutter-sdk-overview), [Unity](unity-sdk-overview), [Kotlin Multiplatform](kmp-sdk-overview), and [Capacitor](capacitor-sdk-overview).

Server-side, Adapty connects to the stores directly, so every renewal, refund, and billing issue reaches you in real time — even when the app is closed. Supported: [App Store](initial_ios), [Google Play](initial-android), [Stripe](stripe), and [Paddle](paddle), plus a [custom integration](custom-store) for any other provider.

### Products, offers, and access levels

Adapty separates what you sell from what users unlock. Because of that split, you can reprice, swap products, or launch offers without shipping an app update. Three pieces make it work:

- **[Products](product)** — one product unifies your App Store, Play Store, and web SKUs — subscriptions, one-time purchases, or consumables — so you manage the catalog in one place.
- **[Offers](offers)** — the levers that move conversion: introductory, promotional, and win-back discounts.
- **[Access levels](access-level)** — allow you to decouple access privileges from individual products.

### Flows and placements

Build the screens that drive revenue — paywalls, onboardings, quizzes — without writing code. [Flows](adapty-flow-builder) render on-device through the SDK, so you can change copy, design, and pricing anytime without an app release. Start from a [template](paywall-builder-templates) or a blank canvas. Attach each flow to a [placement](placements), then target different [audiences](audience) built from [segments](segments).

<ZoomImage width="700px" id="flow-builder/flow-builder-overview.webp" alt="The Adapty flow builder workspace" />

### Profiles and segments

Look up any subscriber to see their full history — [profiles](profiles-crm) hold each user's event timeline, subscription state, revenue, and custom attributes. Slice your user base into [segments](segments) by any attribute, then personalize what users see, filter analytics, and scope A/B tests. The [event feed](event-feed) streams every subscription event as it happens.

### A/B tests and AI Growth Advisor

Grow revenue by finding what converts best:

- **[A/B tests](ab-tests)** — test different prices, trial lengths, and flow designs.
- **[AI Growth Advisor](autopilot)** — tells you exactly what to A/B test next. Benchmarks your paywall against 20,000+ subscription apps and ranks experiments by expected revenue lift. See [how it works](autopilot-how-it-works).

### Analytics and Predictions

[Analytics](analytics) turns store, SDK, and attribution data into a real-time revenue dashboard with [dozens of metrics](metric-comparison-table) — many more than the App Store and Google Play show on their own. ## Adapty Ads Manager

[Adapty Ads Manager](adapty-ads-manager) is an Apple Ads platform. It replaces the native Apple Ads console with AI-driven optimization, real-time revenue attribution, and competitive intelligence. Because Core already tracks every install, trial, subscription, and renewal, Ads Manager connects ad spend directly to LTV. No MMP required.

Key features:

- **[Campaigns and keywords](ads-manager)** — build and manage them, along with ad groups and bids, from the Adapty dashboard.
- **[AI Agent](ads-manager-ai-agent)** — plain-language full-funnel queries and recommendations.
- **[Market Intelligence](ads-manager-market-intelligence)** — competitor keyword strategies across 50+ countries.
- **[CPP A/B Tests](ads-manager-cpp-ab-tests)** — custom product pages compared head-to-head.
- **[Automations](ads-manager-automations)** — your campaigns optimize themselves. Bids, keywords, and search terms adjust automatically when your metrics cross set thresholds.

## Adapty Attribution

[Adapty Attribution](adapty-user-acquisition) matches app installs and subscription revenue to the ad campaigns that drove them. It combines ad-platform spend, tracking-link clicks, and SDK install events into ROAS, LTV, and cohort views across your paid channels. No external MMP required.

Key features:

- **[Ad-platform integrations](ua-integrations)** — Meta Ads, TikTok for Business, FunnelFox, and S3/GCS pipes.
- **[Tracking links](ua-tracking-links)** — generated in Adapty, added to your campaigns, and matched to installs at first launch.
- **[Deferred deeplinks](ua-deferred-data)** — route new users to the right in-app content on first launch, even if they clicked before installing.
- **[Attribution data](ua-attribution-data)** — receive the attribution payload in your app for custom logic.

## Adapty Mail

[Adapty Mail](adapty-mail) turns user data into AI-generated email campaigns. It builds a [brand profile](mail-brand) from your app's store listing, landing page, and social profiles, then generates a full email sequence in minutes. Emails are sent from your verified domain, and each purchase is attributed to the email that drove it. No separate email platform required.

Key features:

- **[Campaigns](mail-email-campaigns)** — a complete multi-email sequence, generated in one pass. It starts sending once you attach it to a flow.
- **[Flows](mail-flows)** — bind a campaign to a segment and a subscription-event trigger like *never purchased* or *billing issue*, so it sends automatically.
- **[Web paywall](mail-checkout)** — personalized checkout pages, one per recipient, so purchases attribute back to the email.
- **[Segments](mail-segments)** and **[profiles](mail-profiles)** — reach the users most likely to convert. Build a segment from purchase state, country, or revenue, then trigger a campaign or flow off it. The data comes from Adapty Core, limited to identified profiles with an email.

## The wider ecosystem

Two more products connect to your Adapty data but serve needs outside the core lifecycle.

### FunnelFox

[FunnelFox](https://funnelfox.com/docs/integrations/subscription-management/adapty) is a web-to-app funnel builder. It creates landing pages and quizzes that drive shoppers to your app. Its [billing](https://funnelfox.com/docs/billing/integration-billing-funnelfox) engine collects their payments on the web. Connect FunnelFox to Adapty for subscription tracking and revenue attribution.

### Adapty Finance

[Adapty Finance](https://adapty.io/blog/introducing-adapty-finance/) advances your future subscription revenue, so you skip the wait for store payouts.
