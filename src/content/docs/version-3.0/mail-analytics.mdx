The Analytics dashboard shows real-time performance across all your campaigns. Use it to monitor how your sequences drive engagement and revenue, identify which emails convert best, and compare versions in A/B tests. Metrics are organized into three categories: delivery, engagement, and revenue. Dashboard data updates within a few minutes of email events.

## Delivery metrics

| Metric | What it shows |
|---|---|
| Emails sent | Total emails dispatched across all sequences |
| Delivered | Emails that reached the inbox |
| Bounced | Emails that failed delivery — see [Unsubscribe and suppression](mail-suppression) |
| Spam complaints | Recipients who marked your email as spam |

## Engagement metrics

| Metric | What it shows |
|---|---|
| Open rate | Share of delivered emails that were opened (pixel-based tracking). Note: Apple Mail Privacy Protection on iOS 15+ pre-fetches email content, which inflates this metric — use click rate and revenue as more reliable signals |
| Click rate | Share of delivered emails with at least one link click |
| Unsubscribes | Recipients who opted out |

## Revenue metrics

| Metric | What it shows |
|---|---|
| Checkout completions | Purchases completed via email checkout links |
| Revenue | Total revenue attributed to email-driven purchases |
| Revenue per email | Revenue breakdown by individual email in the sequence |

Revenue is attributed using the `customer_user_id` embedded in each checkout link. When a purchase completes, Adapty matches it to the specific email whose link the user clicked (last-click attribution).

## Filter and analyze

Use the controls at the top of the Analytics page to filter:

- **Campaign version**: Compare different sequence versions side by side.
- **Individual email**: See metrics for a specific email in the sequence.
- **Date range**: Set a custom period or use presets.

## Compare versions

If you're running multiple versions of a campaign, see [A/B testing](mail-ab-testing) for how to read version-level comparisons and deploy a winner.
