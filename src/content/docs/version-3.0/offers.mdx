Offers are discounts or free trials for auto-renewable subscriptions in the App Store and Google Play. They help attract new users, re-engage churned subscribers, and increase conversion.

## Enable offers in your app

The setup depends on the offer type and store.

### App Store

- **Introductory offers** — [configure in App Store Connect](app-store-offers#introductory-offers). Adapty detects them automatically.
- **Offer codes** — [configure in App Store Connect](app-store-offers#offer-codes). Adapty detects them automatically.
- **Promotional and win-back offers** — [configure in App Store Connect](app-store-offers#promotional-offers), [upload your subscription key](app-store-connection-configuration#step-4-for-trials-and-special-offers--set-up-promotional-offers), then [add the offers to Adapty](create-offer).

### Google Play

[Configure offers in Play Console](google-play-offers), then [add them to Adapty](create-offer).
