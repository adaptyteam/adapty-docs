## Prerequisites

- **App Store Connect access** — AI Growth Advisor benchmarks against App Store data. [Upload your App Store Connect credentials ](set-up-app-store-connect) before starting your first analysis.
- **At least one priced App Store product on the selected paywall** — paywalls configured for Google Play only can't be analyzed. Add an App Store product in the **Products** section before starting.
- **Paywall placement** — [flow](adapty-flow-builder) placements are in beta and can't be analyzed by AI Growth Advisor yet.
- Note: AI Growth Advisor works for all apps — but the more financial data Adapty can analyze, the more reliable the recommendations.

## How AI Growth Advisor Works

:::link
The [AI Growth Advisor: How it works](autopilot-how-it-works) article explains how Adapty generates its recommendations.
:::

1. AI Growth Advisor [analyzes your paywall's content and design](autopilot-analysis#paywall-analysis), highlights things that work, and detects **opportunities for conversion improvement**.
        - Recommendations compare your design to patterns from top-performing apps in your category.
        - The analysis focuses only on subscription products. Consumable in-app purchases are not included.
2. Across your top markets, AI Growth Advisor [recommends per-country price adjustments](autopilot-growth-plan#geo-pricing-hypotheses) and [compares your conversion to similar apps](autopilot-analysis#market-and-competitor-analysis).
        - Conversion benchmarks draw on anonymized data across the 20,000-app Adapty network.
        - Pricing recommendations draw on the [Adapty Pricing Index](https://uploads.adapty.io/adapty_pricing_index.pdf), a country-level benchmark of what subscribers actually pay.
3. After the analysis is complete, AI Growth Advisor presents an [actionable growth plan](autopilot-growth-plan#view-the-growth-plan) for the selected placement. Each hypothesis is an A/B test to try out a new approach. As market conditions change, you can re-run the analysis and expand your plan with new hypotheses.
4. [Run the suggested tests](autopilot-execute-plan) one by one. Based on our estimates, a comprehensive set of AI Growth Advisor tests could increase your revenue **by up to 80%**.

## Run the analysis

:::link
Main article: [Paywall and Market Analysis](autopilot-analysis)
:::

1. Open the **AI Growth Advisor** page and click the [Get Growth plan](https://app.adapty.io/ab-tests/analysis/start) button.

        <ZoomImage id="pricing-analysis.webp" width="700px" />

2. [Select a Placement and Paywall](autopilot-analysis#select-a-paywall-for-analysis). Confirm that the products displayed are correct. Upload a screenshot and click **Confirm & Analyze**.

        <ZoomImage id="autopilot-select-placement.webp" width="600px" />

3. Adapty [analyzes](autopilot-analysis#paywall-analysis) your paywall's design, content, and revenue metrics. Review the report to find out what's working and what needs improvement.

        <ZoomImage id="autopilot-paywall-diagnostic.webp" width="600px" />

4. [Select up to 5 competitors](autopilot-analysis#select-competitors) for an in-depth comparison. Click **Generate report**.

        <ZoomImage id="competitors.webp" width="700px" />

5. Read the [market insights](autopilot-analysis#market-and-competitor-analysis) report. It compares your paywall's performance against competitors and country-specific market benchmarks. You can switch between different countries to change the context of the analysis.
    * Geographic revenue distribution
    * Competitor pricing (country-specific)
    * Conversion funnel benchmarks (country-specific, market average)
    * Revenue distribution by subscription duration (country-specific, market average)
    * Activation ARPU (country-specific, market average)

        <ZoomImage id="autopilot-industry-benchmark-complete.webp" width="700px" />

## Manage your growth plan

:::link
Main article: [Manage your growth plan](autopilot-growth-plan)
:::

6. Open the [growth plan](autopilot-growth-plan#view-the-growth-plan) and review Adapty's data-driven hypotheses. Each is an A/B test that targets a single pricing or design change. Add your own hypotheses to expand the plan.

        <ZoomImage id="growth-plan.webp" width="700px" />

7. To keep your growth plan relevant, regularly [re-run the analysis](autopilot-growth-plan#get-new-ai-generated-hypotheses). Each re-run pulls in fresh market data and adds new hypotheses on top of your existing plan.

## Execute your growth plan

:::link
Main article: [Execute your growth plan](autopilot-execute-plan)
:::

8. [Run the tests](autopilot-execute-plan) one by one. Create new products, duplicate your paywall with the suggested changes, and launch the A/B test. The winner setup will participate in the next test.

        <ZoomImage id="hypothesis-wizard-step-1.webp" width="700px" />
