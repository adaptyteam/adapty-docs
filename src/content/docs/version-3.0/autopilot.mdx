## How Autopilot Works

:::link
The [Growth Autopilot: How it works](autopilot-how-it-works) article explains how Adapty generates its recommendations.
:::

* Autopilot [analyzes your paywall's content and design](autopilot-analysis#paywall-analysis), highlights things that work, and detects **opportunities for conversion improvement**.
        - The analysis is based on data from the Apple App Store.
        - The analysis focuses only on subscription products. Consumable in-app purchases are not included.
* It pulls country-specific **market data** for your app category, [compares your app](autopilot-analysis#market-and-competitor-analysis) to its competitors, and [detects pricing mismatches](autopilot-growth-plan#geo-pricing-hypotheses) in different markets.
        - Competitor research reports are country-specific. This allows us to understand the differences across markets.
* After the analysis is complete, Autopilot presents an [actionable growth plan](autopilot-growth-plan#view-the-growth-plan). Each hypothesis is an A/B test to try out a new approach. Based on our estimates, completing the entire test cycle could increase your revenue **by up to 80%**.

## Run the analysis

:::link
Main article: [Paywall and Market Analysis](autopilot-analysis)
:::

1. Open the **A/B tests** page and click the [Get Growth plan](https://app.adapty.io/ab-tests/analysis/start) button.

        <ZoomImage id="pricing-analysis.webp" width="700px" />

2. [Select a Placement and Paywall](autopilot-analysis#select-a-paywall-for-analysis). Confirm that the products displayed are correct. Upload a screenshot and click **Confirm & Analyze**.

        <ZoomImage id="autopilot-select-placement.webp" width="600px" />

3. Adapty [analyzes](autopilot-analysis#paywall-analysis) your paywall's design, content, and revenue metrics. Review the report to find out what's working and what needs improvement.

        <ZoomImage id="autopilot-paywall-diagnostic.webp" width="600px" />

4. [Select up to 5 competitors](autopilot-analysis#select-competitors) for an in-depth comparison. Click **Generate report**.

        <ZoomImage id="competitors.webp" width="700px" />

5. Read the [market insights](autopilot-analysis#market-and-competitor-analysis) report to learn about:
    * Geographic revenue distribution
    * Competitor pricing (country-specific)
    * Conversion funnel benchmarks (country-specific, market average)
    * Revenue distribution by subscription duration (country-specific, market average)

        <ZoomImage id="industry-benchmarks.webp" width="700px" />

## Manage and execute your growth plan

:::link
Main article: [Manage and execute your growth plan](autopilot-growth-plan)
:::

6. Open the [growth plan](autopilot-growth-plan#view-the-growth-plan) and review Adapty's data-driven hypotheses. Each is an A/B test that targets a single pricing or design change. Add your own hypotheses to expand the plan.

        <ZoomImage id="growth-plan.webp" width="700px" />

7. [Run the tests](autopilot-growth-plan#run-the-tests) one by one. Create new products, duplicate your paywall with the suggested changes, and launch the A/B test. The winner setup will participate in the next test.

        <ZoomImage id="hypothesis.webp" width="700px" />

8. To keep your growth plan relevant, regularly [update](autopilot-growth-plan#get-new-recommendations) the analysis. As market conditions change, suggestions may become outdated.
