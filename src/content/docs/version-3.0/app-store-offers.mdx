The App Store supports two ways to offer subscription discounts or trials:

- [Offers](#offers) are discounts or free trials applied automatically for eligible users. There are three offer types, each targeting a different user group.
- [Offer codes](#offer-codes) are redeemable codes that provide a temporary discount or free trial. Users redeem them manually. You configure offer codes entirely in App Store Connect — no Adapty setup needed.

Adapty supports both.

## Offers

There are three offer types in the App Store:

- **[Introductory offers](#introductory-offers)**:
  - Free or discounted subscription periods
  - Only users who haven't previously subscribed or used an introductory offer are eligible
  - No Adapty configuration needed — applied automatically for eligible users
- **[Promotional offers](#promotional-offers)**:
  - Free subscription periods, percentage discounts, fixed price discounts
  - Any user can be eligible
  - Requires setup in Adapty: add the offer to your product and paywall. Adapty handles the signing and applies the offer at purchase time.
- **[Win-back offers](#win-back-offers)**:
  - Free subscription periods or percentage discounts
  - Only churned users are eligible
  - Requires setup in Adapty: add the offer to your product and paywall. Adapty handles the signing and applies the offer at purchase time.

:::important
[Upload your subscription key](app-store-connection-configuration#step-4-for-trials-and-special-offers--set-up-promotional-offers) to enable promotional and win-back offers.
:::

### Introductory offers

Adapty automatically applies introductory offers for eligible users.

To enable introductory offers, create them in App Store Connect:

1. Open your app in App Store Connect and switch to **Monetization > Subscriptions**.
2. Select a subscription group and navigate to the subscription you need. The subscription must have a duration configured.
3. Click **View all Subscription Pricing** and switch to the **Introductory offers** tab. Click **Set up introductory offer**.

<ZoomImage id="apple-intro-offer.webp" />

4. Select countries and regions where the introductory offer will be available.

<ZoomImage id="apple-countries-intro.webp" />

5. Select start and end dates for the introductory offer. If the introductory offer has no specific end date, select **No end date**. Click **Next**.

<ZoomImage id="apple-dates-intro.webp" />

6. Select the introductory offer type. Depending on what you select, you also need to define the offer duration and price. Read more in the [Apple documentation](https://developer.apple.com/help/app-store-connect/manage-subscriptions/set-up-introductory-offers-for-auto-renewable-subscriptions).

<ZoomImage id="apple-type-intro.webp" />

7. Review your selection and click **Confirm**.

As you finish the setup, you don't need to do anything in Adapty. The offer will be applied when an eligible user encounters a paywall with the product.

### Promotional offers

To use promotional offers, set them up in App Store Connect and add them to your Adapty product and paywall. Adapty applies the offer at purchase time:

1. Open your app in App Store Connect and switch to **Monetization > Subscriptions** from the left menu.
2. Select a subscription group and navigate to the subscription you need. The subscription must have a duration configured.
3. Click **View all Subscription Pricing** and switch to the **Promotional offers** tab. Click **Set up promotional offer**.

<ZoomImage id="apple-promo-offer.webp" />

4. Set the promotional offer details. These values can't be changed after creation.
   - **Promotional offer reference name**: The promotional offer name. It won't be visible to your users.
   - **Promotional offer identifier**: The promotional offer identification code. You will use it to add the offer to Adapty.

<ZoomImage id="apple-id-promo.webp" />

5. Select the offer type: **Pay as you go** or **Pay up front** for a discount, or **Free** for a free subscription period. Then set the offer duration and price. Read more in the [Apple documentation](https://developer.apple.com/help/app-store-connect/manage-subscriptions/set-up-promotional-offers-for-auto-renewable-subscriptions).

<ZoomImage id="apple-type-promo.webp" />

6. If needed, set different prices for different countries and regions and click **Next**.

<ZoomImage id="apple-price-promo.webp" />

7. Review your choice and click **Confirm**.

8. [Add the offer](create-offer) to Adapty.

### Win-back offers

:::important
Before you can create a win-back offer, your subscription must be approved by App Review.
:::

To use win-back offers, create an offer in App Store Connect and add it to your product and paywall in Adapty. Adapty applies the offer at purchase time for eligible users:

1. Open your app in App Store Connect and switch to **Monetization > Subscriptions** from the left menu.
2. Select a subscription group and navigate to the subscription you need. The subscription must have a duration configured.
3. Click **View all Subscription Pricing** and switch to the **Win-back offers** tab. Click **Create offer**.
4. Set the win-back offer details. These values can't be changed after creation.
  - **Reference name**: The offer name. It won't be visible to your users.
  - **Offer identifier**: The offer identification code. You will use it to add the offer to Adapty.
5. Configure the offer type, duration, and price. Read more in the [Apple documentation](https://developer.apple.com/help/app-store-connect/manage-subscriptions/set-up-win-back-offers).
6. Review your choice and click **Confirm**.
7. [Add the offer](create-offer) to Adapty.

## Offer codes

:::link
Do not confuse with the deprecated [promo code functionality](#legacy-promo-codes-deprecated)
:::

Offer codes allow you to give discounts or free trials to specific users. Unlike regular offers that are applied automatically, offer codes are distributed outside the app — through email campaigns, social media, or printed materials. Users redeem them by entering the code in the App Store, following a redemption URL, or through an in-app dialog.

To set up offer codes, open a subscription in App Store Connect and go to its **Offer Codes** section. You can create [three kinds](https://developer.apple.com/help/app-store-connect/manage-subscriptions/set-up-subscription-offer-codes) of offer codes:

- **Free** — the subscription is free for a set duration, and the next renewal is at full price.
- **Pay as you go** — the user pays a discounted price each billing cycle for a set duration, then the subscription renews at full price.
- **Pay up front** — the user pays a single discounted price for the entire offer duration, then the subscription renews at full price.

You don't need to add offer codes to Adapty. Apple tags every transaction during the offer period with the offer code category. This includes the initial redemption and all subsequent discounted renewals. Adapty detects the tag and records each transaction with the offer category `offer_code`. Once the offer period ends and the subscription renews at full price, the tag is no longer present. You can filter analytics by the **Offer Code** offer type in the [Adapty Dashboard](controls-filters-grouping-compare-proceeds).

To let users redeem offer codes inside your app, display the code redemption sheet. See [Redeem offer codes in iOS](making-purchases#redeem-offer-codes-in-ios) for implementation details.

### Revenue discrepancy troubleshooting

If you notice that an offer code transaction appears in Adapty at the full product price instead of the discounted offer price, verify the following in App Store Connect:

- The offer code has the correct pricing configured for all regions where users can redeem it.
- The offer price is set for the specific country or region of the user. Apple sends the regional price in the transaction. If no regional price is configured for the offer, Apple may send the full product price instead.

You can filter and verify offer code transactions in the [Adapty Dashboard](controls-filters-grouping-compare-proceeds) by the **Offer Code** offer type and **Offer Discount Type** filters.

### Legacy promo codes (deprecated)

:::warning
Apple deprecated promo codes for in-app purchases in March 2026. Offer codes replace them with more capabilities: configurable eligibility, expiration dates, and up to 1 million codes per quarter. If you previously used promo codes for in-app purchases, transition to offer codes in App Store Connect.
:::

Legacy promo codes (limited to 100 per app per version) granted free access to a subscription. Unlike offer codes, Apple did not include discount information in promo code transactions — it sent the full product price in the receipt. As a result, Adapty recorded these transactions at the full price, which caused revenue discrepancies between Adapty analytics and App Store Connect.

If you see historical transactions at full price that should have been free, they are likely from legacy promo codes. Since these codes are now deprecated, transition to offer codes for accurate revenue tracking.
