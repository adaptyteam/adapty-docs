Adapty Mail needs two pieces of data per user: their email (for delivery) and their `customer_user_id` (for personalized checkout links and purchase attribution). Both must reach Adapty before you launch a campaign.

## Identify your users

Each user must be identified with a stable ID before their email is passed. Call `Adapty.identify()` with a backend user ID, Firebase UID, or similar — any ID that persists across sessions and reinstalls.

- [iOS](identifying-users)
- [Android](android-identifying-users)
- [React Native](react-native-identifying-users)
- [Flutter](flutter-identifying-users)
- [Unity](unity-identifying-users)
- [Kotlin Multiplatform](kmp-identifying-users)
- [Capacitor](capacitor-identifying-users)

:::important
Always call `Adapty.identify()` with a stable ID — not an anonymous identifier. If a user uninstalls and reinstalls your app, Adapty needs this ID to link the reinstall to the existing profile and attribute any purchase back to the correct user.
:::

Call `Adapty.identify()` at login or app initialization — before any paywall is shown. Call `updateProfile()` with the email immediately after the user provides it.

## Pass the email to Adapty

After obtaining the user's email, pass it to Adapty via `updateProfile` using the `email` parameter.

- [iOS](setting-user-attributes)
- [Android](android-setting-user-attributes)
- [React Native](react-native-setting-user-attributes)

:::important
Ensure you have explicit user consent before collecting and passing emails to Adapty. You are responsible for compliance with GDPR, CAN-SPAM, and other applicable regulations in your target markets.
:::

## Email collection strategies

Most apps don't collect emails by default. Here are five approaches, roughly ordered from least effort to most.

### Apps with existing authentication

If users sign up with email/password, Firebase, Supabase, social login, or magic links, you already have their email. Pass it to Adapty after login.

| Auth method | How you get the email |
|---|---|
| Email + password | User enters it directly |
| Firebase Auth | `FirebaseAuth.instance.currentUser.email` |
| Supabase Auth | `supabase.auth.currentUser.email` |
| Google Sign-In | Provided in the auth response |
| Apple Sign-In | Provided in the auth response (may be a relay address — still deliverable) |
| Magic link / OTP | User enters email to receive the link |

**Best for**: Apps that already have any form of authentication.

### Email gate before the paywall

Add an email prompt immediately before showing the paywall. No full authentication required — just a single email input screen.

Example flow:
1. User completes onboarding.
2. App shows an email input screen.
3. User enters their email — app sends it to Adapty.
4. Paywall is shown.

Conversion at this step typically ranges from 70–90%, because users have already invested time completing onboarding and an email prompt feels natural before payment.

**Best for**: Apps without authentication — health, wellness, astrology, photo editors.

### FunnelFox checkout

If you use FunnelFox for checkout, the first checkout screen collects the user's email on the web. No in-app code is required for this step.

This approach covers users who receive a campaign email before an in-app email gate is in place. When they click the checkout link and complete the funnel, their email is passed to Adapty automatically.

**Best for**: Minimal SDK work; email collected on web, not in the native app.

### Onboarding step

Add an email input to your multi-step onboarding, ideally after users have invested time — for example, after completing a quiz or setting goals.

Tips for higher completion:
- Frame the request as value delivery: "We'll email your personalized plan."
- Place the email step after 2–3 engaging steps, when users are most committed.
- Avoid making the step skippable if possible.

**Best for**: Quiz-based onboarding (fitness, nutrition, astrology, education).

### Server-side API import

For users migrating from another email platform or re-engaging a historical list, use Adapty's server-side API to import profiles with emails already set.

Use the [Update profile](api-adapty/operations/updateProfile) endpoint with the `email` field in the request body. Pass the user's `customer_user_id` as `external_profile_id` to match the record to an existing Adapty profile.

**Best for**: Apps with an existing email list that want to run campaigns before completing SDK integration.

## Verify your email coverage

After implementing collection, check coverage in Adapty:

1. Go to **Customers → Profiles**.
2. Filter by profiles that have an email set.

Target at least 30–50% email coverage among your active users before launching your first campaign.

:::tip
You don't need to wait for 100% coverage. Launch campaigns as soon as you reach 30% — users who provide their email later are automatically enrolled in active campaigns when they qualify.
:::

## Anonymous and partially-identified users

If your app supports anonymous users, they cannot receive campaigns until they are identified with a stable ID. Identify them when they create an account or log in — from that point, any email they provide is matched to their Adapty profile.

## Users without an email

Users without an email on their Adapty profile are excluded from campaign delivery and don't appear in campaign analytics. As soon as they provide an email, they become eligible for future campaigns.
