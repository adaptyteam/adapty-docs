The Trial Toggle is a binary switch used to swap between standard and trial-based product states. It triggers On Changed actions to update pricing, variables, or visibility of product groups instantly.

A Trial toggle is a regular container with "Turn into selectable element" enabled. No special type — just a container that belongs to a group and gets a Selected state.
