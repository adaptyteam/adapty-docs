The **User Engagement** element category provides four templates for showcasing reviews, ratings, and social proof on a paywall. Each template is a fully editable composition — replace the placeholder text and apply your [color styles](builder-styling) and [typography](onboarding-text) to match the rest of the flow.

<ZoomImage id="user-engagement-templates.webp" width="500px" alt="User engagement templates: Review, Rating, App Rating, Social Proof" />

## Review

A card with a single rating, quote, and author byline. Use to feature a memorable user quote.

## Rating

A count and star row, like "17000+ rating". Use to emphasize the volume of ratings.

## App Rating

A featured score with sample size, like "4.9 / Based on 1000+ reviews". Use to highlight a strong overall score.

## Social Proof

An avatar group with a member count, like "Join 50,000+ users". Use to emphasize community size.
