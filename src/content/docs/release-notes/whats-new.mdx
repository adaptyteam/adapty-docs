Discover the latest features, improvements, SDK updates, and documentation enhancements that help you optimize your app's monetization strategy. This page highlights the most important releases each month.

## July 2026

- **Virtual currencies**: Define in-app currencies like tokens, coins or gems, grant and track a balance for each user, and read those balances from your server through the server-side API. [Learn more](virtual-currencies)

- **AI Agent in Apple Ads Manager**: Ask an advisory chat agent about your Apple Ads performance and get answers drawn from your campaign data, without building reports by hand. [Learn more](ads-manager-ai-agent)

- **New automations in Apple Ads Manager**: Automate changes at the campaign and ad group levels with two new rule types, alongside the existing keyword and search term automations. [Campaign rules](ads-manager-automations-campaign-rules) | [Ad group rules](ads-manager-automations-ad-group-rules)

- **Profiles in Adapty Mail**: A per-subscriber view that shows each user's journey, current subscription state, and unsubscribe status in one place. [Learn more](mail-profiles)

- **SDK v4 for React Native, Flutter, Capacitor, and Kotlin Multiplatform**: The Flows-enabled v4 SDKs are here. React Native, Flutter, and Capacitor reached general availability, and Kotlin Multiplatform v4 launched — each with its own migration guide. [React Native](migration-to-react-native-sdk-v4) | [Flutter](migration-to-flutter-sdk-v4) | [Capacitor](migration-to-capacitor-sdk-v4) | [Kotlin Multiplatform](migration-to-kmp-sdk-v4)

- **New webhook fields**: Webhook payloads now include the original price and discount for each transaction, so you can track promotional and introductory pricing downstream. These fields are available in webhooks only. [Learn more](webhook-event-types-and-fields)

- **Strikethrough prices in flows**: Show a crossed-out original price next to the discounted one, with a discount badge, directly in the Flow Builder. [Learn more](strikethrough-price)

- **Flow template gallery**: Start a new flow from a professionally designed template instead of a blank canvas, then customize it to match your app. [Learn more](paywall-builder-templates)

- **Install tools button**: Every documentation article now has an **Install tools** button in the header. It opens a modal with copy-ready commands to install the Adapty SDK integration skill in Claude Code, Copilot CLI, Gemini CLI, Codex, and other AI coding assistants. [Learn more](adapty-sdk-integration-skill)

- **New Unity SDK installation method**: You can now install the Unity SDK through Swift Package Manager, with added troubleshooting guidance for common setup issues. [Learn more](sdk-installation-unity)

- **Footer container in the Flow Builder**: A sticky bottom panel that stays pinned while the rest of the screen scrolls — a fit for CTA buttons, legal text, and links. [Learn more](builder-containers#footer)

- **New Flow Builder video tutorials**: A growing YouTube playlist of step-by-step walkthroughs for building flows, now embedded across the Flow Builder guides. [Learn more](adapty-flow-builder)

## June 2026

- **Flows now support Android**: The visual no-code builder for paywalls and onboardings now works on Android SDK v4 and up, alongside iOS. Screens render natively, with no web views. [Learn more](adapty-flow-builder)

- **CPP A/B Tests in Apple Ads Manager**: Compare custom product pages against each other inside Apple Ads. Pick 2 to 4 pages — including your current default — and Apple Ads rotates traffic between them and reports which one converts best. [Learn more](ads-manager-cpp-ab-tests)

- **Adapty Mail API**: Send user profiles and transactions to Adapty Mail directly from your server, without routing data through the Adapty SDK. Use it to seed a subscriber base, reuse subscribers from your other apps, or keep your backend as the source of truth. [Learn more](mail-send-data-via-api)

- **Show an Apple Ads-targeted paywall on first launch**: Apple Ads attribution arrives after the SDK activates, so a paywall requested too early misses your Apple Ads audience. Use `AdaptyProfile.appliedAttributionSources` to show the Apple Ads-targeted paywall as soon as attribution data arrives. [iOS](ios-show-aa-targeted-paywall) | [React Native](react-native-show-aa-targeted-paywall) | [Capacitor](capacitor-show-aa-targeted-paywall)

- **Autosave in the Flow Builder**: The Flow Builder now saves your progress automatically once a minute, so you no longer lose unsaved work when you leave the page. You can still save a draft manually with **Cmd/Ctrl + S**. [Learn more](builder-save-publish)

- **New Flow Builder video tutorials**: Two new walkthroughs cover building navigation between flow screens and designing element states such as selected, active, and disabled. [Navigation in flows](onboarding-navigation-branching) | [Element states](builder-element-states)

- **Documentation in Japanese and Vietnamese**: The Adapty documentation is now available in Japanese (日本語) and Vietnamese (Tiếng Việt). Switch languages using the language selector in the top navigation.

## May 2026

- **Flows (Beta)**: Create entire screen sequences in a visual no-code builder — single-screen paywalls, multi-step onboardings, and anything in between, all in one flow. Screens render natively without web views, and you can update copy, design, and logic without shipping an app release. Currently supports iOS, Android, React Native, Flutter, and Capacitor SDK v4 and up. [Learn more](adapty-flow-builder)

- **Autopilot now adapts to your test results**: Acting as an AI growth manager, it updates the growth plan after every completed round. The next hypothesis builds on which experiments you've run, which ones won, and which directions are still worth exploring — instead of following a fixed sequence. [Learn more](autopilot-how-it-works#how-ai-growth-advisor-decides-what-to-recommend)

- **Activation ARPU in Autopilot Market Insights**: A new chart compares your app's average revenue per new install against the category average. Pair it with the conversion funnel — high conversion combined with a low Activation ARPU may indicate underpriced offers. [Learn more](autopilot-analysis#activation-arpu)

- **Analytics in Adapty Mail**: Compare delivery metrics and email-attributed revenue for each campaign in one view. Group, break down, and filter by campaign, segment, A/B variant, message, or trigger, then drill into any row. [Learn more](mail-analytics)

- **Brand profile in Adapty Mail**: A consolidated profile that drives email copy, tone, visuals, and web paywall content. Adapty builds it from your app's store listing, landing page, legal pages, and social profiles, and you can review or refine every section inline. [Learn more](mail-brand)

- **Predictions in Adapty UA**: Predicted revenue, ROAS, ad profit, ARPU, and ARPPU for each cohort, so you can compare campaigns before they have time to mature. Predictions are built from your app's own historical cohort data, updated daily, and available at cohort periods from D0 through D360 or a custom day. [Learn more](ua-predicted-metrics)

- **New fields in Adapty UA custom S3 export**: The custom S3 export now includes `bundle_id`, `device_brand`, `device_model`, `os_version`, `app_version`, and `sdk_version`. Slice and join attribution data by device and app version downstream. [Learn more](ua-custom-s3)

- **Placement audiences in the CLI**: The `adapty placements create` and `adapty placements update` commands now take an `--audiences` flag — a JSON array of `{segment_ids, paywall_id, priority}` entries — so you can target different paywalls to different segments from the terminal. The new `adapty paywalls placements` command lists every placement that uses a given paywall, so you can preview the impact before swapping it. [Learn more](developer-cli-reference#placements)

- **Documentation in Spanish**: The Adapty documentation is now available in Spanish (Español). Switch languages using the language selector in the top navigation.

## April 2026

- **Adapty Mail**: AI-generated email campaigns that turn trial users into paid subscribers. Build, send, and attribute campaigns from your Adapty project — no separate email platform required. [Learn more](adapty-mail)

- **Paywall Diagnostic in Autopilot**: Find out what to fix on your paywall before you build a test. Upload a screenshot and Autopilot returns benchmark-driven recommendations from top-performing apps in your category, plus AI-generated layout and copy suggestions. The benchmarked ones become A/B test rounds in your growth plan. [Learn more](autopilot-analysis#paywall-analysis)

- **Clearer guidance for every Autopilot suggestion**: Each hypothesis now spells out why it matters (a data-driven explanation of how your paywall deviates from established patterns), what to change and how to set up the A/B test, and which metrics to watch in a new "How to interpret your results" section. [Learn more](autopilot-execute-plan#step-1-view-the-hypothesis)

- **Keep your Autopilot growth plan current**: Refresh the analysis to pull in the latest market data and new suggestions, and revisit past suggestions from the version history if the new ones miss the mark. Hypotheses are grouped into Top priority, All, Pricing, Visual, Geo-pricing, and Archived tabs. [Learn more](autopilot-growth-plan)

- **Revenue distribution by duration in Autopilot**: See whether your revenue is overconcentrated in one subscription duration. A new Market Insights chart shows your revenue mix by duration alongside the industry average for your category and country. [Learn more](autopilot-analysis#revenue-distribution-by-duration)

- **Updated LTV and revenue predictions**: Predicted LTV and revenue now use your app's own cohort retention data when there's enough history, and cross-app averages otherwise — so even newer apps get usable forecasts in analytics and A/B tests. [Learn more](predicted-ltv-and-revenue)

- **Send all events in Adapty UA**: Give Meta and TikTok a fuller picture of conversions for sharper audience modeling. Adapty now supports forwarding installs and transactions from organic and non-attributed users to your pixel, not just users matched to a campaign. [Meta](ua-facebook#send-all-events) | [TikTok](ua-tiktok#send-all-events)

- **Documentation in Russian and Turkish**: The Adapty documentation is now available in Russian (Русский) and Turkish (Türkçe). Switch languages using the language selector in the top navigation.

## March 2026

- **Developer CLI**: Manage your Adapty account from the terminal without opening the Dashboard. The CLI lets you create apps, define access levels, set up products, create paywalls, and configure placements — all scriptable for automated environments. An [Adapty CLI skill](https://github.com/adaptyteam/adapty-cli/tree/main/skills/adapty-cli) is also available to help AI coding assistants work with the CLI. [Learn more](developer-cli)

- **Overview page in Apple Ads Manager**: See all key Apple Ads metrics in one place, each with a trend chart. Filter by app using the header dropdown, customize which metrics appear, and adjust the chart type and revenue display. [Learn more](ads-manager-overview)

- **Market Intelligence in Apple Ads Manager**: See which keywords your competitors run ads on across 50+ countries, and add top-performing keywords directly to your campaigns. [Learn more](ads-manager-market-intelligence)

- **Full-cycle keyword automations in Apple Ads Manager**: Automatically adjust bids, pause or activate keywords, and move them between ad groups based on performance rules you define. [Learn more](ads-manager-automations-keyword-rules)

- **Bid history in Apple Ads Manager**: View the full change log for any keyword's CPT bid — when each change happened, the previous and new values, and which automation rule triggered it. [Learn more](ads-manager-manage-keywords#bid-history)

- **Visual rounds in Autopilot**: Paywall design suggestions are now first-class rounds in your growth plan — listed in the sidebar alongside monetization rounds. Each visual round includes a design mockup, a description of when the pattern works best, and the key metrics it targets. [Learn more](autopilot-growth-plan#view-the-growth-plan)

- **Add your own hypothesis to Autopilot**: Extend your growth plan with custom rounds. Add a title, description, round type (monetization or visual), target metrics, and — for monetization rounds — the products involved. [Learn more](autopilot-growth-plan#add-your-own-hypothesis)

- **Reorder Autopilot rounds**: Drag and reorder the stages of your growth plan to run experiments in the order that fits your strategy best. [Learn more](autopilot)

- **Geo-pricing rounds in Autopilot**: Test country-specific price changes as a new type of round in your growth plan. Based on Market Insights data, Autopilot recommends whether to increase, decrease, or maintain prices in each country. Add a recommendation as a geo-pricing round to run it as an A/B test — up to 5 can run simultaneously. [Learn more](autopilot-growth-plan#geo-pricing-hypotheses)

- **Search term automations in Apple Ads Manager**: Automatically promote winning search terms to exact-match keywords and negate them at the source — without manual report downloads. Rules can be created from templates or built from scratch with custom conditions and schedules. [Learn more](ads-manager-automations-search-terms)

- **Maximize Conversions bidding in Apple Ads Manager**: When creating campaigns, you can now select Maximize Conversions as the bidding strategy. Apple's algorithm maximizes downloads within your budget, guided by an optional target CPA. [Learn more](ads-manager-create-campaign)

- **FunnelFox integration in Adapty UA**: New integration with FunnelFox is now available in Adapty UA. [FunnelFox](ua-funnelfox)

- **Documentation in Chinese**: The Adapty documentation is now available in Chinese (中文). Switch languages using the language selector in the top navigation.

## January 2026

- **Capacitor SDK officially released**: The Capacitor SDK is now production-ready after extensive testing. Build subscription apps for iOS and Android using Capacitor with full Adapty integration support. [Learn more](capacitor-sdk-overview)

- **Autopilot for new apps**: Autopilot analysis is now available even if your app doesn't have extensive transaction history yet. Get data-driven price optimization recommendations and create your growth plan from day one. [Learn more](autopilot)

- **Global pricing opportunities in Autopilot**: Identify revenue potential across your top-performing markets with country-specific pricing recommendations. Autopilot analyzes conversion rates and purchasing power for your next top 5 countries, providing data-driven insights on whether to increase, decrease, or maintain prices based on the Adapty Pricing Index. [Learn more](autopilot)

- **Billing recovery conversion metrics**: New analytics charts track revenue recovered from billing issues and grace periods. Monitor "Billing issue converted", "Billing issue converted revenue", "Grace period converted", and "Grace period converted revenue" to measure your retention recovery efforts.

- **Direct ad management in Apple Ads Manager**: Create and manage your Apple Ads campaigns directly within Adapty without switching between platforms. [Learn more](ads-manager-manage-ads)

- **Apple Ads Manager analytics**: Access detailed ad-level performance metrics and attribution data within Adapty. View campaign performance, ad group analytics, and attribution insights in a unified dashboard. [Learn more](adapty-ads-manager-analytics)

- **Apple Ads attribution charts**: Combine multiple attribution metrics in customizable charts to analyze your Apple Ads performance alongside subscription data. [Learn more](adapty-ads-manager-analytics#charts)

- **Apple Ads attribution segments**: Create user segments based on Apple Ads attribution data with a streamlined two-click workflow. Target users by campaign, ad group, or keyword for more precise analysis and experiments. [Learn more](ads-manager-create-segments)

- **New documentation platform**: The documentation site has been migrated to a new platform, enabling faster feature updates and improved user experience with enhanced search, navigation, and content organization.
